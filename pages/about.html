The Identity 🎯

Fusing Heritage & Innovation

Camp21: Synergising Literacies 2026 is an intensive national innovation sprint designed for Politeknik Malaysia cohorts[cite: 4, 8].

The Three Core Pillars

Pillar 01

Malaysian
Culture 🎨

Immersing teams within active artisan contexts directly inside Kompleks Kraf Kuala Lumpur to discover traditional craft motifs and preserve heritage storytelling[cite: 8, 10, 89].

Pillar 02

Design
Thinking 🧠

Moving systematically across a human-centered design pipeline: Empathise, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test to turn cultural data into real-world solutions[cite: 12, 14, 55].

Pillar 03

AI Co-Pilot
Empowerment ⚡

Leveraging generative prompt engineering responsibly across ChatGPT and Canva AI environments to accelerate brainstorming and enhance structural concept posters[cite: 12, 55, 62].

The Executive Mandate

Jointly organized under the official authority of JPPKK and METrO KL, this immersive camp challenges 10 selected teams to align their innovative outputs with global Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 4, SDG 11, and SDG 12)[cite: 4, 5, 44].
